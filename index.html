Taylor Wessing

Corporate rebrand

Taylor Wessing

Corporate rebrand

A global law firm with headquarters in London and Frankfurt


Functioning as a small agency within the firm, the practical application of all deliverables - photography, graphics, internal comms and templates - became paramount, considering the myriad layers of sign-off synonymous with law firms.

Our solution was based on the evolution of previously proposed line graphic patterns - taking influence from the work of Hartmut Böhm - drawing parallels between the organic and elegant patterns found within nature, and Taylor Wessing’s refined and logical working processes and reputation.

Outputs included a range of bold line graphic devices and patterns, sector specific iconography, and photographic suites featuring geometric lines and patterns for generic usage, and close-up detail of relevant objects set against new brand colours for more specific subject matters. Both suites were designed to balance and contrast against a new clean typographic design and generous use of white space.

With the unfortunate events surrounding Covid-19, our brand launch plans were completely revised, and I proposed a staff-access micro-site designed to educate and celebrate Taylor Wessing’s new brand direction remotely, allowing the website to tastefully lead as the sole new brand promotional tool. This was launched in May 2020.

Gibraltar

Nation branding and EUFA application

Gibraltar

Nation branding and EUFA application

A British Overseas Territory on the Iberian peninsula


Produce concepts for 2 branding projects; support and promote the Gibraltar Football Association’s bid for membership of UEFA; create a 'nation branding' proposal for all government departments.

Recognising UEFA as 53 member organisation, we adopted a presumptuous campaign approach by labelling Gibraltar as 'Team 54', celebrating the nation's long standing enthusiasm for football, and unique rich cultural heritage.

We proceed an adaptable brand identity to be applied across all government departments and agencies - including tourism, finance, education and maritime - while simultaneously safeguarding the nation’s brand identity to revive in it a proud sense of statehood, with a unified clear, and contemporary identity.

Our campaign won the Grand Prix Overall Winner at the Drum Marketing Awards 2014, and in May 2013 51 nations voted ‘Yes’ to grant Gibraltar EUFA membership.
