GAVIN VAN ROOD
About me

Born and based in London, with qualifications in Graphic Design and Interior Design I loosely describe myself as a Conceptual Creative, working with branding agencies, marketing teams and individual clients to provide design concepts, strategy, management and roll-out.

Influenced by architecture, construction, textiles and anthropology, I believe good design solutions come from persistence, planning, teamwork and encouragement.

Throughout my career I’ve adapted and evolved my skillsets, allowing for a fluid transition between a variety of disciplines including:

Graphic Design & Branding, Art Direction and Interior Design.





hello@gavinvanrood.com

Graphic Design & Branding

With a degree in graphic design, I’ve worked across multiple sectors producing work that includes logo design, illustration, typography, print ads, point-of-sale, brochure layout & design, social media ads, commercial signage and internal way-finding.





Art Direction

Working in a classic Art-director / Copywriter duo team, I’ve helped to produce numerous pitch proposals, ad campaigns, refine brand’s tone of voice and created brand guidelines - leading creative direction and assuming brand guardian responsibility with an enthusiastic hands-on approach. Making good use of my background in graphic design, I create clean & considered presentation docs, PSD visualisations and digital mock-ups.





Photoshoot Art Direction

Taking my creative skills into the physical world and public spaces, I’ve worked on shoots for corporate branding, fashion styling and most recently the leisure hotel market as part of a wider branding campaign, creating a consistent look for over 300 unique properties. From sourcing props to dressing rooms and partnering with photographers, I’ve always enjoyed being hands on, and on-location.





Interior Design

Following recent studies in interior design, I’ve taken my domestic practice of this field into the commercial sphere, leading the creative direction in collaboration with Conran Interiors, for a suite of rental apartments in London’s Old St. Combining my skills in technical & perspective drawing with a love for patterns, textures, space zoning and furniture sourcing, I create creative solutions from colour scheme concepts to accurate plans and 3D renders.
