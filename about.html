About



This portfolio is a collection of projects spanning my career as a conceptual creative and qualified Graphic Designer, specialising in branding, marketing and interior design.

Born and based in East London I operate through a limited company, working with branding agencies and marketing teams to provide strategy, planning, design concepts, management and roll out - collaborating with an established network of individuals from interior designers to developers, product designers to event organisers, marketeers and copywriters.

With an interest in architecture, construction, textiles and anthropology, I believe good design and productive solutions come from persistence, planning, teamwork and encouragement.
