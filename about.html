Brand design, Graphics & Art Direction

I am a conceptual Designer and Art Director with experience in integrated campaigns, brand design and brand strategy, working freelance, agency and client side in both London and Sydney. Working across across print, environmental, experiential and social media, I manage delivery & rollout, client relationships, planning and photo shoots.

Described as hard working and accommodating with a sharp eye for creative detail and a critical, questioning mind, I gain inspiration from textiles and patterns, typography, anthropology, architecture and interior design.

With a modern, sophisticated and playful style, I believe good design and productive solutions come from persistence, planning, teamwork and encouragement.
