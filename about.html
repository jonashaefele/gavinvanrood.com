GAVIN VAN ROOD
About

A collection of projects spanning my career as a conceptual creative specialising in graphic design, branding, marketing and interior design.

Born and based in East London, with qualifications in graphic design & interior design, I work with branding agencies, marketing teams and individual clients to provide strategy, planning, design concepts, management and roll-out. Collaborating with an established network of copywriters, developers, interior designers, product designers and event organisers.

Influenced by architecture, construction, textiles and anthropology, I believe good design solutions come from persistence, planning, teamwork and encouragement.
