GAVIN VAN ROOD

Empire Warehouse

Branding, signage and interior design

A brand new flagship Apart-Hotel within a fully restored Victorian Warehouse located in the heart of London’s Bankside.

To gather a full understanding of the site, I obtained archaeological reports from the Museum of London, joined local tours and conducted research into the local area; threading these findings into property logo options, neighbourhood guides and apartment artwork.

Working alongside architects and interior designers, I curated communal spaces including the Guest Pantry and Lobby area, using materials and furnishings carefully selected to sympathetically blend with traditional and new architectural additions, while simultaneously creating exterior signage and internal way-finding solutions.

A curated guest experience was established throughout the building at all customer touch-points, as well as within new public spaces including a grand lobby, guest’s lounge and pantry.
