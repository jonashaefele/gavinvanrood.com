This page is an ‘image dump’ of sorts, documenting a selection of freelance work produced from the first lockdown to present day.

The nature of freelance design has increased my adaptability, flexibility and knowledge base, working for a variety of agencies from 3 day to 3 month placements with some amazing talent across London and beyond. Whether working independently to develop brand identities from concept to launch, working with established brand guidelines for quick turnaround on-brand deliverables, or creative collaborations varying length and size, I've helped to produce brand identities, campaign concepts and pitch presentations.

As a result some of the work displayed here is collaborative, some are creative artwork projects, and others are purely my own.
