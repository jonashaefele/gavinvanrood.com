Taylor Wessing - Global law firm

Corporate rebrand

Functioning as a small agency within the firm, the practical application of all deliverables - photography, graphics, internal comms and templates - became paramount, considering the myriad layers of sign-off synonymous with law firms.

Our solution was baesd on the evolution of previously proposed line graphic patterns - taking influence from the work of Hartmut Böhm - drawing parallels between the organic and elegant patterns found within nature, and Taylor Wessing’s refined and logical working processes and reputation.

Outputs included a range of bold line graphic devices and patterns, sector specific iconography, and photographic suites featuring geometric lines and patterns for generic usage, and close-up detail of relevant objects set against new brand colours for more specific subject matters. Both suites were designed to balance and cintrast against a new clean typographic design and generous use of white space.

With the unfortunate events surrounding the Covid-19 pandemic, our brand launch plans were completely revised, and I proposed a staff access micro-site designed to educate and celebrate Taylor Wessing’s new brand direction remotely, allowing the website to tastefully lead as the sole new brand promotional tool. This was launched in May 2020.
