Taylor Wessing - Global law firm

Corporate rebrand

Functioning as a small agency within the firm, the practical application of all deliverables - photography, graphics, internal comms and templates - was identified as the driving force behind much of the project’s direction, taking into consideration the myriad layers of sign-off synonymous with law firms.

Our solution was to evolve a series of line graphic patterns - that took influence from etc work of Hartmut Böhm - drawing parallels between the organic and elegant patterns found in nature, and Taylor Wessing’s refined, logical yet similarly organic processes and approach to staff, clients, and their own legal sectors.

From this direction came a range of bold line graphic devices and patterns, and a full suite of photographic imagery with a focus on geometric lines and patterns serving as elegant and beautiful backdrops, balancing the clean, uncluttered typography and generous use of white space. A 2nd suite featuring close-up detail of relevant objects against the new brand colour palette to accommodate for more speciic subject matters.

A suite of universally recognisable, yet unique and unexpected sector icons were also created, allowing for balance or interaction with imagery in the same manner as the line graphics.
