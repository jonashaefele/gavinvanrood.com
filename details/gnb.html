Go Native

Brand development and marketing

The UK's largest boutique Serviced Apartment operator.

We were tasked to develop and refresh the existing Go Native brand, increase market awareness, and implement the ‘neighbourhood included’ USP, - reflecting local knowhow, carefully selected locations and unique properties.

As part of a integrated rebrand project, reposition and evolve the existing brand identity, streamline b2b communications, produce an e-commerce online presence, and support a launch into the consumer market

Collaborating with copywriters and photographers, I refined the tone of voice, and Art Directed a series of lifestyle / property photoshoots, establishing a new, clean visual face for the company to include launch events, internal comms, digital promotions, advertising, property branding and street level signage.

The strap-line ‘Why book a hotel when you can Go Native?’ supported the product, and cross integrated campaigns promoted ‘spaces to live cook, work and relax and entertain’, as a strong voice in the leisure market. New Art Directed property photography has helped to generate a feeling of being in the space, more appealing and down to earth.

Online bookings and market awareness increased substantially, attracting huge international investment and expansion.
