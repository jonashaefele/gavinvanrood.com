Virgin Atlantic

2013 Sales conference

Brief

Propose a creative concept to their UK’s annual sales conference, 2013; Theme to motivate staff and encourage ‘overcoming adversity’, reflecting the event’s content; Considerate of the client’s restricted budget; Deliverables include Power Point templates, invitations and event decoration.

Solution

Big idea: Extreme adventures and explorers; Realised with the use of high impact photography across all comms; Language and slogans tailored to the event theme.

Impact

Inspirational messaging theme and content worked seemlessly throughout the day; All charts, quotes and support material communicated effectively; Positive feedback from client, and sales staff output improved in the following year.
