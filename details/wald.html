GAVIN VAN ROOD

The Waldorf Hilton

Brochure design

A legendary hotel and club in London’s East End, dating from 1908.

Design and produce a standalone piece to celebrate the Waldorf’s 100 year anniversary.

Opting to design outside of the Hilton brand guidelines, I proposed a visual timeline of key events throughout the period, and editorial style features celebrating prominent moments in the hotel's history. Extensive research into historical archives - coupled with site visits, interviews, and photography - created a rich source of content, which I curated into a high quality book that respectfully reflected the hotel’s historical importance and outstanding architecture.

Available for purchase in the hotel lobby, the book was extremely well received, and produced with foil-block detail and embossed hardback cover.
