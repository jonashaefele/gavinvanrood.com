St Edmund Homes

Brand identity

A newly established British construction firm specialising in residential developments.

Design a distinct logo and brand direction to be used in investment proposal documents and future promotional comms.

Citing Edmund as the King of East Anglia in the 9th century and the first Patron Saint of England, I proposed a series of logo options with neutral nods to the past, delicately avoiding regal connotations. Adopting the traditional wax seal process to create a strong motif with contemporary applications and colour palettes, the heritage contrasts naturally with the clean design.

Well received, the firm's launch and design proposals are on hold until investment is finalised.
